ACUMEN

keenness and depth of perception, discernment, or discrimination especially in practical matters

Examples

  • Here was a man of extraordinary sensitivity, political acumen, spiritual power, a free spirit if ever there was one.
  • Her political acumen won her the election.
  • a lack of business acumen

Synonyms Anonyms
astuteness, caginess, canniness, clear-sightedness, foxiness, hardheadedness witlessness
