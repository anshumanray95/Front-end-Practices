EDIFY

to instruct and improve especially in moral and religious knowledge

Examples

  • These books will both entertain and edify readers.
  • A family-oriented show that tried to edify the television audience as well as entertain it
  • Next week, the senior center is hosting a weeklong affair to edify its members.

Synonyms Anonyms
educate, enlighten, illume, illuminate cold, unfeeling, unfriendly, unkind
