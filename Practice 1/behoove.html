BEHOOVE

to be necessary, proper, or advantageous for

Examples

  • Does it really behoove IndyCar to turn away willing and paying participants?
  • These guys would all behoove themselves to just stay off Twitter.
  • Crown them with coal nuggets, handling Such antiquity as behooves it, For out of this darkness, light.

Synonyms Anonyms
befit, become, suit, be fitting to disagree, violate, defuse, unmuzzle
