eCommerce

Ecommerce or electronic commerc, a subject of ebusiness, ecommerce is the purchasing and exchanging of goods and services over computer networks (such as the internet) throught which transactions or terms of sales are performed electronically.

eCommerce requirements

Shopping cart Merchant account
Processing Gateway Digital Certificate
Ecommerce can be broken into four main categories
  • B2B(Business-to-Business)
  • B2C(Business-to-Company)
  • C2B(Company-to-Business)
  • C2C(Company-to-Company)
